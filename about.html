About Me

Driven by Design,
Powered by Code.

Hello! I'm Himasha Jayasinhe, a passionate Web Developer and Graphic Designer based in Sri Lanka. My journey in the digital world began with a curiosity for how things work on the internet, which quickly evolved into a dedicated career in building beautiful, functional websites.

With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS, I create responsive and performant web applications. I specifically focus on clean, efficient frontend solutions that deliver exceptional user experiences.

Beyond coding, I have a keen eye for aesthetics. My skills in Photoshop allow me to bridge the gap between design and development, ensuring that every pixel is perfect. I also specialize in WordPress, helping clients manage their content with ease.
